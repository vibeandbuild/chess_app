How to move

Click a piece and then a highlighted square, or drag it. While Stockfish is thinking, you can set one premove and it will show in red. Right-click a square to mark it red, or right-drag to draw arrows. Left click clears board annotations. Use the on-screen arrows or your keyboard's left and right keys to review the game.
